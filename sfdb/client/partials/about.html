The San Francisco Database is a catalogue of new and major construction projects in San Francisco. It includes residential, office, city infrastructure, parks, and other attractions in the area.

I put sfDB together to better understand the flurry of recent changes in San Francisco, as well as to practice my web development skills.

This site uses these technologies: HTML5, CSS3, SASS/SCSS, AngularJS + libraries, Angular Google Maps, Bootstrap, and packaged with Gulp. In addition to these, I can also work with jQuery, vanilla JavaScript, and any graphics editor. Contact me if you would like to know more about me or the sfDB.
